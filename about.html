About OpenSky News

OpenSky News is an independent digital publication focused on geopolitics, defense, technology, markets, and strategic analysis.

We report on how power, policy, and technology intersect — from global summits and military developments to semiconductor supply chains, satellite intelligence, and emerging economic shifts.

Our work blends factual reporting with contextual analysis, aiming to explain not just what is happening, but why it matters.

OpenSky News operates with an editorial approach grounded in publicly available information, open-source research, and verified reporting. In an era of rapid information flow and competing narratives, we prioritize clarity, accuracy, and restraint over speed or speculation.

Our coverage is written by a combination of staff writers and analysis contributors. Some pieces are reported news, while others are labeled analysis to distinguish interpretation from straight reporting.

OpenSky News is reader-supported and independent. We do not accept payment to publish or suppress stories, and editorial decisions are made without influence from advertisers, governments, or political organizations.
