Code of Ethics

OpenSky News is committed to responsible, independent journalism. Our reporting and analysis follow clear ethical principles designed to maintain accuracy, fairness, and editorial integrity.

We strive to ensure that information published on this site is accurate, verified, and presented in appropriate context. When errors occur, we seek to correct them promptly and transparently.

Editorial decisions at OpenSky News are made independently. We do not accept payment, gifts, or incentives in exchange for coverage or favorable treatment.

Our content distinguishes clearly between reported news and analysis or opinion. Analysis pieces are intended to provide context and interpretation, not advocacy.

We rely primarily on publicly available information, open-source research, and verified reporting. We respect privacy, avoid unnecessary harm, and consider the public interest in all editorial judgments.
