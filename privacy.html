Privacy Policy

OpenSky News respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using this website.

Cookies may be used to support basic site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended.

Links on OpenSky News may direct you to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

We take reasonable measures to protect user data but cannot guarantee absolute security. By using this site, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the Contact page.
